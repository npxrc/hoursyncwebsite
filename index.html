Auto Login

HourSync works with the eHours portal just as ClassLink works with Canvas, Flex, and StudentVue. Sign in once and HourSync will sign you in automatically. Every time.†

Drafts

We all know the pain of submitting a lengthy eHours request, only to have it lost since you were signed out. HourSync saves your progress as you go, so you can come back to it later.

AI Assistant ‡

HourSync has an optional, built-in AI sidebar of your choice when submitting eHour requests, so it becomes a breeze to write a description of your eHours.
